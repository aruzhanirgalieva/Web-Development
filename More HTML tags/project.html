Pasta

  • Time: 20 minut
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 150 g
Unpilled shrimps 300 g
Parmezan cheese 30-40 g
Cream with a fat content of 20% 180 ml

Step 1: Remove head and shell from shrimp. Make a small incision along the top of the shrimp to remove the intestinal vein. Rinse the shrimp again if necessary and pat dry.
Step 2:Pasta needs to be boiled in plenty of water so that it does not stick together, so take a pan that is suitable in size. Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente, slightly undercooked.
Step 3:Seafood sauce can be prepared in parallel with pasta. Peel the garlic, cut into slices. Lightly heat olive oil and butter in a skillet. Simmer the garlic over moderate heat for 1 minute to fragrant the oil. Remove the garlic from the pan.
Step 4:Put the seafood in the pan. Fry them over moderate heat, turning occasionally, about 3 minutes, until golden brown. Pour cream into the pan, add heat. Add grated cheese to the sauce, mix. Cheese will give the sauce a more viscous consistency and make the taste more intense. When adding salt to the sauce, consider the saltiness of the cheese.

Pizza

  • Time: 45 minut
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 1 tablespoon
sliced 1 onion
talian bread crumbs ¼ cup
steak sauce ¼ cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and set aside.
Step 2: Place pork and beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, parsley, garlic, oregano, onion, sugar, basil and salt. Simmer over medium-low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 3: In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, eggs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt and pepper.
Step 4: In a 9x13 inch baking dish, place 2 layers of noodles on the bottom of dish; layer 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese and 1/2 of the sauce; repeat layers.
